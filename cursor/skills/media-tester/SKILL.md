# Media-Tester — Video

**check_types**: [video]

## Analysis Criteria

Analyse the screenshot and accessibility tree for:

**Video Issues:**
- Video player not loading
- Play button not working
- Video controls missing or broken
- Sound not working or muted by default
- Video not loading (infinite buffering)
- Quality settings not working
- Fullscreen button broken
- Captions/subtitles not available
- Video thumbnail not loading
- Autoplay issues
- Video obscuring important content
- Mobile video playback issues
