# Product-Inspector — Product Details

**check_types**: [product-details]

## Analysis Criteria

Analyse the screenshot and accessibility tree for:

**Product Details Issues:**
- Product images not loading or broken
- Missing product specifications
- Price display issues or missing price
- "Add to cart" button not working or missing
- Size/variant selection broken
- Product description truncated or missing
- Review display issues
- Stock availability not shown
- Image zoom not working
- Missing product metadata (SKU, brand, etc.)
- Broken product image gallery
